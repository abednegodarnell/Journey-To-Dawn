# Journey to Dawn 🌅

Journey to Dawn is a survival RPG where you explore a relaxing yet challenging world. Build your home, fight monsters, and survive the night to see the dawn. Your new adventure begins now.

---

## More About the Game

Journey to Dawn is a survival-RPG hybrid set in a relaxing yet challenging world. As an adventurer, your goal is simple: forge your own path, build your homestead, defeat the creatures of the night, and survive to see the dawn.

*   **Survive & Thrive:** Balance relaxation with the constant challenge of nature.
*   **Base Building:** Gather resources and construct your own home.
*   **Action-RPG Combat:** Battle monsters that threaten your survival.
*   **Craft Powerful Gears:** Obtain powerful gears, or.. manage the crops in your farms.
*   **More Features Coming Soon..**
